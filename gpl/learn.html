Your Playmat

Deck:Each player's 60 card deck goes here.

Active Spot:This spot is where eah player put down their Pokemon that will be active during game play.

Bench: This is where each player can play upto 5 addtional pokemon

Prize:Each player place 6 cards from their deck, face down in this section. Players pick up a card after knocking out their opponent's pokemon.

Discard:Any card that gets discarded during game play goes here.
