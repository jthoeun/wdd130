Content

Home page

The home page will where visitors will be greeted with pictures of game play. The goal of this site is to be a hub where information relating to the Gooding Pokemon Leauge is shared to the community and the world. This page will also serve as a main point to find information about the league and the tutorial of the Pokemon trading card game. There will be a navigation section with links to the tutorial and about us pages. The goal of the site is to be a place for the local community to find out about the league, and information that that anyone can find. There will also be a link that take visitors to the public library so that visitors know that the league is associated with the library. There will also be a section with a brief information about meeting time along with a picture from Google Maps showing where the library is located.

Images for the Home page

How to Play

The How to Play page is where visitors can learn how to play the Pokemon Trading Card game. This page will have pictures of the main phase of gameplay. There will also be a section on the page that shows parts of a card and how to read them. Another section will have a external links to Pokemon.com to find more information about the card game. The goal of this page is to get visitors introduced to the card game and see pictures of games play in action. This will also give a view of what happens in actual gameplay verses reading illustrated pictures in a small book. The page will also be an place where it is easy to follow along if visitors would like to follow as they play their own game at home. Each picture will be an explanation of what is going on in the picture. There will be another section of how to build a good deck and a sample template will be provided in this section so that visitors can follow along. This section will also tips from players from the league on this topic.

Images for the Page 2

About Us

The about us page is where visitors can read about the league. This will be divided into 3 sections. First section is the “Who are we section”. This will briefly cover a brief bio of the people who are running the league and why they got into pokemon and the card game. The second section will be “About the league”. This section will cover information about the league. Some of the information that will be covered will include a history of the league, where to find the league and meeting time. This information will be the most important piece that most visitors interested in visiting would want to find. The last section is the Contact information. This will have contact information for the league will also be displayed here so visitors can reach out if they have any questions. There will also be a link on this page redirecting to the league’s official page on Pokemon’s official site where visitors can see a calendar of when league in that month.
